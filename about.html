Advocate is redefining
democracy.

Advocate is a New York and San Francisco based technology startup working to transform politics by building a revolutionary platform connecting citizens with their elected officials, creating new habits around always-on citizenship.

Our team believes that money has too great of an impact over the political process, amplifying the wishes and wants of a wealthy minority and disenfranchising the common citizen. We are working to build tools for a modern society and to restore transparency and accountability back into Democracy.

Advocate enables politicians to reward supporters with a direct window into the inner-workings of the political process in exchange for their advocacy, valuing amplified engagement in the political process over financial contributions.

Our end goal is to create an always-on political environment where politicians and their constituents can interact daily throughout the political process, not solely when it is election season.
